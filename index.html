Brokerage Folder Reorganization

One shared home per file. Organized by what a document is, never by who works on it. People and territories change; vendors, retailers, and functions stay put.

Sponsor: Felix Villa  |  Domestic lead: Jeff Niedholdt  |  Technical owner: Nikita Bahmet  |  Updated Jul 13, 2026

Why we are doing this

The Brokerage library grew for years with no rules. Duplicates everywhere, files buried under old rep names, nobody sure where the current version lives. This resets it to a clean, numbered structure with one named owner per folder who keeps it tidy.

No owner by design

The five rules

  • One home per file. Links or shortcuts elsewhere, never copies.
  • No dates in filenames for living docs. Use SharePoint version history and edit in place.
  • Templates & forms live inside each main folder in a "Templates & Forms" subfolder. Copy out and rename the moment you use one.
  • Drafts stay in personal OneDrive or WORKSPACES, not the shared tree.
  • Every top-level folder has one named owner who sweeps it quarterly.
